Kalashnikov Concern

Kalashnikov Concern is one of the largest and most well-known defense manufacturers in Russia. The company was founded in 1807 in the city of Izhevsk and became internationally famous after the development of the AK-47 rifle in 1947.

Today, Kalashnikov produces a wide range of modern military equipment, including rifles, drones, and advanced protection systems. Its products are known for their reliability, durability, and simple design.

About

ZONTIK-1 is a next-generation active protection system designed to detect, track, and neutralize incoming threats. It combines advanced radar monitoring with rapid-response countermeasures to improve survivability and defensive capability in high-risk environments.
